## What is Pair Programming? What are the roles and how do they work?

Pair Programming means two programmers working together, where one is a drivr and other is a Navigator. the roles will be reversed after certain time span.

THe benefits exist in all stages as both programmers will learn the thought process of solving the problem/process of coding. In the worst case, the learning curve to pick and continue is close to nill.
